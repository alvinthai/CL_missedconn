Missed Connections is a section of Craigslist where people write about strangers they notice, generally in an attempt to reconnect. These posts vary from lewd & salacious banter, to romantic fairy-tales, and describe many different scenarios. This project explores common themes and writing styles across age and sexual orientation subgroups.


  • outlet for expressing regrets and frustrations so people can move on with their lives
  • express how you really feel without judgment and nothing to lose (safety in anonymity)
  • ways to relive encounters
  • vulnerability, hopes & dreams
